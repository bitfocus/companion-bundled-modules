## Bolin PTZ

This connection controls PTZ cameras from [Bolin Technology](https://bolintechnology.com).

## Configuration

- Enter the IP address of your device into the connection settings.
- If you have not manually changed the HTTP port on your camera, you can leave the port at the default of 80.
- Enter the username and password of your device into the connection settings.
- It is recommend to start by using the provided Presets. There are presets that integrate actions, feedbacks, and variables for every function of the camera that is controllable by the connection.
